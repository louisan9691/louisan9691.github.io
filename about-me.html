A FEW WORDS ABOUT ME

I’m an eLearning resource designer. I was born in Vietnam, brought up in Singapore and currently working in Melbourne. I am a details oriented and user focused developer, who enjoys minimal design and in love with UI/UX aspects of design for the web, tablet and mobile. I am extremely passionate about web development and helping people to improve their current websites or completely start fresh. I am a coffee lover who enjoys travelling and reading books.

Let’s meet up and have a friendly talk over a cup of coffee.


Over the past 3 years, I have been aspired to create designs that are not only aesthetic and functional, have a passion to transform education by enhancing learning and teaching experience. I love to design and develop the resources that appeal, engage and focus on student-centred. I also enjoy working with teachers by collaborating in design learning activities and supporting them in using technology to enhance their teaching practice.
